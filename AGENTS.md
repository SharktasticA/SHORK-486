# agents.md

This repository and all software, documentation, and assets under the **SHORK** family (comprising SHORK 486, SHORK Utilities, and SHORK Entertainment) operate under a strict **No-LLM (Large Language Model) Policy**. 

We believe in the craftsmanship, integrity, and direct understanding of human-written code. To preserve the engineering quality of this project, this document establishes clear boundaries for all contributions and external dependencies.

## 1. Core Policy

No code, configuration, documentation, comments, or commit messages controlled or published directly under the SHORK family may be generated by, assisted by, or filtered through a Large Language Model (LLM) or automated AI coding assistant (including Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or locally-trained models).

*   **No Generated Code:** All systems logic, scripting, and software architecture must be authored directly by human engineers.
*   **No Vibecoding:** Code must be deeply understood, structured, and implemented manually. Submitting logic generated via descriptive prompts without absolute technical mastery over the output is prohibited.
*   **No Generated Documentation:** Readmes, internal architecture files, inline documentation, and deployment guides must be written entirely by human authors.

## 2. Pull Request & Contribution Guidelines

Every Pull Request (PR) and contribution submitted to this project is subject to verification before merging. 

*   **Immediate Denial:** Any PR or contribution found to contain LLM-generated code, AI-authored documentation, or hallmarks of machine-assisted "vibecoding" will be denied immediately without exception.
*   **Automated Agents Banned:** Automated PRs, documentation updates, or commits initiated by LLM bots or AI agents are completely banned from the repository lifecycle.
*   **Contributor Responsibility:** By submitting a PR, you certify that every line of code, configuration, and prose you contribute was written by a human without the assistance of generative AI.

## 3. Scope, Third-Party Software, and Learning Sources

While we maintain absolute control over our host repositories, we recognize the realities of the broader software ecosystem:

*   **Third-Party Bundled Software:** We cannot guarantee that upstream dependencies, external libraries, or third-party bundled software comply with this policy. However, we choose our dependencies carefully to minimize exposure.
*   **Educational Materials:** The tutorials, reference documentation, and source materials we utilize for research may have been influenced or generated by LLMs unbeknownst to us. 
*   **Our Promise:** We will exercise maximum vigilance to recognize, filter, and reject LLM-influenced inputs in our research to ensure it does not compromise the infrastructure and code we directly control.

Even when considering locally-hosted models or limiting usage to speeding up repetitive boilerplate tasks, **our goal is for the SHORK family to remain a 100 percent human-made project.**
